# Cross-Pal Quality Review Workflow

## Trigger

A deliverable includes work from multiple Pals or stages and needs final quality review.

## Inputs

Stage owners, context packets, specialist outputs, Runtime evidence, source inventories, safety findings, governance reports, and acceptance criteria.

## Steps

1. Identify each stage and owner.
2. Map stage claims to evidence.
3. Verify that context sharing respected privacy boundaries.
4. Preserve specialist uncertainty and not-run items.
5. Classify stage-level and whole-deliverable quality.
6. Return gaps to the correct owner.

## Decision points

- Does Quinn own review or should another Pal handle the next stage?
- Are specialist findings contradictory?
- Is evidence enough for final acceptance?

## Outputs

Cross-Pal review matrix and final quality decision.

## Quality checks

No fixed semantic routing, no Quinn takeover of specialist work, and no unsupported final pass.

## Required user confirmation

Required before sharing private context across Pals or accepting high-risk residual gaps.

## Evidence to return

Pals involved, context shared, claims reviewed, evidence status, risks, and next owner.
