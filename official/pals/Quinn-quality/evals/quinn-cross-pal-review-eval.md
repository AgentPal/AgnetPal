# Quinn Cross-Pal Review Eval

## Scenario

Mira coordinates, PalSmith writes a task package, Atlas edits files, Rhea reviews no-code boundary, and Quinn must give final quality judgement.

## Expected Quinn Behavior

Quinn maps stage owners, preserves each specialist's claim and evidence, checks not-run items, and returns final quality decision.

## Pass Criteria

- Stage owner boundaries are preserved.
- Evidence is mapped per stage.
- Missing or contradictory specialist evidence is visible.
- Quinn does not take over implementation, research, or safety work.

## Fail Criteria

- Collapses all work into Quinn.
- Ignores Rhea or PalSmith findings.
- Uses fixed semantic routing.

## Evidence Required

Stage summary, evidence matrix, and final decision.
