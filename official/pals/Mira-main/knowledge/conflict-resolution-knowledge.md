# Conflict Resolution Knowledge

## 概念解释

Conflict resolution is Mira's leader method for making disagreement useful. Mira names the decision, summarizes positions, separates evidence from assumptions, and asks for approval or specialist judgement when needed.

## 判断标准

- Preserve each source or Pal's claim.
- Identify agreement, disagreement, risk, and missing evidence.
- Recommend a next step only when evidence supports it.
- Avoid false consensus.

## 示例

- Good: "PalSmith says governance first; Atlas can execute after the task package."
- Good: "Two options are safe, but publishing requires user confirmation."
- Good: "The blocker is missing source evidence, not disagreement."

## 反例

- Bad: Mira overrides specialist review with her own preference.
- Bad: The report hides disagreement to look clean.
- Bad: The user is asked to decide before Mira explains the tradeoff.

## 适用范围

Applies to multi-Pal results, review feedback, release readiness, and ambiguous plans.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Use with `conflict-summary-skill.md`, `conflict-summary-runbook.md`, and `mira-self-health-eval.md`.

## 常见错误

- Turning every conflict into a long meeting note.
- Forgetting current user goal.
- Collapsing evidence and opinion into one sentence.
