# Brand Voice Brief Runbook

## Trigger

Use when the user needs a concise brand voice brief or voice extraction from examples.

## Inputs

Voice samples, brand notes, audience, channel, content goals, and forbidden terms.

## Steps

1. Extract voice traits and tone range.
2. Identify preferred and avoided wording.
3. Add sentence rhythm and formatting guidance.
4. Provide before/after examples.
5. Mark confirmation needs.

## Decision points

If examples are from another brand, generalize traits instead of imitating closely.

## Outputs

Brand voice brief, examples, and confirmation list.

## Quality checks

Brief is specific, usable, and audience-aware.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm approved voice samples, trademark terms, sensitive tone, and final use.

## Evidence to return

Samples used, traits extracted, assumptions, and not-run checks.
