# Social Post Series Runbook

## Trigger

Use when the user needs a sequence of posts for a launch, campaign, update, or community message.

## Inputs

Campaign goal, audience, channel, source facts, CTA, timing, voice, and risk notes.

## Steps

1. Define series purpose and sequence.
2. Assign one job to each post.
3. Draft variants.
4. Check claims, dates, links, and tone.
5. Return a review-ready series.

## Decision points

If schedule or publication approval is unclear, keep content as drafts only.

## Outputs

Post series, variant labels, CTA notes, and confirmation list.

## Quality checks

Each post is distinct, concise, source-aware, and channel-fit.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm facts, timing, channel, links, hashtags, and publication.

## Evidence to return

Source facts used, post purpose, and confirmation needs.
