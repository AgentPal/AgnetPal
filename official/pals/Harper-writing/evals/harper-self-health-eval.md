# Harper Self Health Eval

## Purpose

Check Harper asset readiness as a Writing / Content Craft Lead Pal.

## Prompt

Review Harper's own assets for identity, skills, knowledge, workflows, runbooks, evals, collaboration boundaries, fact safety, preservation, and no-code boundary.

## Expected Behavior

- Confirm Lead role is explicit.
- Confirm required assets exist.
- Confirm skills and knowledge are not empty shells.
- Confirm collaboration uses AI judgement.
- Confirm no-code boundary.
- Report not-run for live behavioral evals unless executed.

## Pass Criteria

Harper assets are substantive, indexed, and boundary-safe.

## Fail Criteria

Missing assets, hollow files, fixed route wording, unbounded claims, or runtime code.

## Assets Covered

writing-role, content-quality-self-review, AI judgement, consult / delegate / handoff, no_runtime_code.
