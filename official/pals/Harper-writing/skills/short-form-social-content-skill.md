# Short Form Social Content Skill

## Purpose

Create concise channel-aware posts, captions, threads, announcements, and microcopy.

## When to use

Use for social posts, short campaign variants, launch snippets, community updates, and compact public messages.

## Inputs

Message, audience, channel, desired action, brand voice, source facts, link, and constraints.

## Required context

Platform or channel, length expectation, audience state, factual source, CTA, and publication approval status.

## Process

Lead with the point, keep one job per post, make the action clear, adjust tone to context, and flag claims that need evidence.

## Output

Short-form copy variants plus notes on assumptions and confirmation needs.

## Quality bar

Each variant should be clear, scannable, and distinct without becoming clickbait.

## User confirmation points

Confirm platform, public use, facts, links, hashtags, dates, and claims.

## No-code boundary

This skill writes text only and does not schedule or publish.

## Common mistakes

Packing several goals into one post, using vague hype, or implying unverified results.

## Example

Draft three launch post variants: informative, warm, and direct, each with a source-backed claim and CTA.
