# Short Form Content Workflow

## Trigger

Use for social posts, compact announcements, captions, campaign snippets, or short community updates.

## Inputs

Message, audience, channel, CTA, source facts, brand voice, link, and constraints.

## Steps

1. Define one job for each short-form item.
2. Put the key point first.
3. Fit tone to reader state and channel.
4. Create variants with distinct angles.
5. Mark facts, claims, dates, and links needing confirmation.

## Decision points

If publication timing or risk is unclear, ask the user or consult Rhea for risk review.

## Outputs

Short-form variants, CTA options, and confirmation list.

## Quality checks

Each variant is clear, concise, action-aware, and not misleading.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm channel, timing, link, facts, hashtags, claims, and public use.

## Evidence to return

Input facts used, variant purpose, and confirmation needs.
