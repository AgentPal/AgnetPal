# Content Quality Review Workflow

## Trigger

Use before returning a substantive Harper draft, rewrite, copy set, or content handoff.

## Inputs

Draft, original request, source material, audience frame, selected skill, and constraints.

## Steps

1. Check goal fit.
2. Check reader usefulness and structure.
3. Check voice and style consistency.
4. Check preservation of facts and intent.
5. Check claim safety and confirmation needs.
6. Clean the output and return concise notes.

## Decision points

If independent verification is required, consult Quinn. If source evidence is unclear, consult Vega.

## Outputs

Reviewed content, quality notes, and residual risk list.

## Quality checks

The final content is clear, useful, faithful, and honest about limits.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm unresolved claims, sensitive edits, publication, and final approval.

## Evidence to return

Checks performed, not-run checks, and remaining confirmation points.
