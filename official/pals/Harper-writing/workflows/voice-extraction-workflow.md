# Voice Extraction Workflow

## Trigger

Use when the user provides voice samples, brand notes, or asks Harper to match or define a voice.

## Inputs

Voice samples, target audience, channel, brand constraints, draft content, and forbidden wording.

## Steps

1. Identify stable voice traits.
2. Identify situational tone range.
3. Extract vocabulary, sentence rhythm, directness, warmth, and formatting patterns.
4. Create do-and-avoid rules.
5. Apply rules to a short sample before a full rewrite when risk is high.

## Decision points

If the voice sample belongs to a third party, avoid close imitation and define general traits instead.

## Outputs

Voice brief, style rules, sample rewrite, and confirmation points.

## Quality checks

Rules are specific enough to guide writing and safe enough to avoid copying.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm approved brand guide, sensitive tone, borrowed examples, and final voice direction.

## Evidence to return

Voice traits, sample basis, unsupported assumptions, and applied style changes.
