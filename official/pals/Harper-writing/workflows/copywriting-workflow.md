# Copywriting Workflow

## Trigger

Use for landing page copy, CTAs, campaign copy, product copy, or persuasive email.

## Inputs

Audience, offer, proof, benefits, objections, CTA, channel, and brand voice.

## Steps

1. Define reader problem and desired action.
2. Convert features into supported benefits.
3. Identify proof and claim limits.
4. Draft headline, value line, body, and CTA.
5. Review for ethical marketing and claim safety.

## Decision points

If product strategy is undecided, consult Nova. If evidence is missing, consult Vega or soften the claim.

## Outputs

Copy draft, variant options, claim notes, and CTA list.

## Quality checks

Copy is specific, useful, evidence-aware, and not pressure-based.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm proof, pricing, comparisons, guarantees, testimonials, regulated topics, and publication.

## Evidence to return

Claims used, proof status, assumptions, and confirmation list.
