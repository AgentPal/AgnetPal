# Copywriting Persuasive Writing Knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Copywriting connects reader need, concrete value, proof, and action. Persuasion is acceptable when it stays evidence-aware and ethical.

## Judgement Standards

Strong copy is specific, benefit-aware, claim-safe, easy to scan, and honest about proof.

## Examples

- Convert a feature into a reader benefit with a clear CTA.
- Use a headline that names the outcome the product is designed to support.

## Counterexamples

- Guaranteeing results without proof.
- Hiding limitations behind emotional wording.

## Scope

Landing pages, CTAs, product copy, email promos, campaign copy, and offer explanations.

## How This Becomes Skill, Workflow, Or Eval

Feeds copywriting-and-persuasion skill, copywriting workflow, landing page runbook, and copywriting eval.

## Common Mistakes

Confusing excitement with evidence, using formulas mechanically, or making an objective claim without support.
