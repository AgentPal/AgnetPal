# Brand Voice Knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Brand voice is the recognizable pattern of language that expresses a brand's character while serving reader needs.

## Judgement Standards

Brand voice should be documented, consistent, adaptable by tone, and never stronger than evidence or reader context allows.

## Examples

- Define preferred words, avoided words, sentence rhythm, and sample rewrites.
- Use a calmer tone for incident communication within the same brand voice.

## Counterexamples

- Treating brand voice as decoration.
- Applying a playful voice to a sensitive apology.

## Scope

Voice briefs, editorial standards, campaign consistency, product copy, support copy, and cross-channel tone.

## How This Becomes Skill, Workflow, Or Eval

Feeds brand-voice-consistency skill, voice extraction workflow, brand voice runbook, and style voice eval.

## Common Mistakes

Using generic traits without examples, ignoring reader state, or imitating a third-party brand too closely.
