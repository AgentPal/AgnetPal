# Style Voice Knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Voice is the stable character of communication; tone adapts to audience, channel, and situation; style is the practical pattern of words, sentences, terms, and formatting.

## Judgement Standards

Style and voice should be clear, consistent, inclusive, reader-aware, and proportionate to risk.

## Examples

- A support apology can stay warm without becoming casual.
- A developer guide can be friendly while remaining precise.

## Counterexamples

- Copying another brand's voice too closely.
- Using humor in a serious safety message.

## Scope

Voice traits, tone range, terminology, inclusivity, rhythm, formality, and style constraints.

## Common Mistakes

Using adjectives without examples, over-stylizing, or letting tone obscure facts.
