# Editing Rewriting Knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Editing improves clarity, flow, tone, structure, and correctness. Rewriting may transform form more deeply while preserving meaning and evidence.

## Judgement Standards

The output should be clearer and still faithful to source facts, user intent, and voice constraints.

## Examples

- Shorten a dense paragraph without removing the key condition.
- Humanize an AI draft while preserving commitments.

## Counterexamples

- Changing a number during a clarity pass.
- Removing caution language because it sounds less elegant.

## Scope

Line edits, structural edits, tone shifts, humanizing, shortening, expansion, and rewrite comparisons.

## How This Becomes Skill, Workflow, Or Eval

Feeds editing-and-rewriting skill, editing workflow, humanizing runbook, and editing rewriting eval.

## Common Mistakes

Polishing before understanding, over-writing the user, or failing to report material changes.
