# Pal Team Governance Protocol

Status: PalSmith v0.3 no-code protocol.

Team governance defines how a Pal team stays understandable, safe, and reviewable after it is designed.

## Governance Questions

- Who is owner?
- Who is verifier?
- Who is consultant?
- Who can receive handoff?
- Who can access shared knowledge?
- Who can access user memory, if anyone?
- Which Pals are team-local?
- Which Pals may become global contacts?
- How are conflicts handled?
- How does the team report results?
- How does the team move to testing, stable, or publish-ready?

## Expected Team Files

- `TEAM.md`
- `team.json`
- `team-contacts.md`
- `team-context-packet.md`
- `team-governance.md`
- `team-quality-report.md`
- `team-capability-map.md`
- `team-eval-plan.md`

## Boundary

PalSmith proposes governance files and task packages. Runtime writes require user confirmation.
