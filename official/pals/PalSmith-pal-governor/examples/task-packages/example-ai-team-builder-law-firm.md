# Example AI Team Builder: Law Firm

User goal: "帮我搭建一个律师事务所 AI 团队。"

## PalSmith Proposal

I recommend starting with 4 Pals:

1. Intake Pal: collects matter type and user-provided facts.
2. Research Pal: organizes research questions and source needs.
3. Drafting Pal: drafts non-final documents and checklists.
4. Risk Verifier Pal: checks uncertainty, jurisdiction boundaries, and escalation needs.

Owner: Intake Pal.

Verifier: Risk Verifier Pal.

Boundary: this team does not provide legal advice or replace a licensed lawyer. It prepares organized drafts and questions for professional review.
