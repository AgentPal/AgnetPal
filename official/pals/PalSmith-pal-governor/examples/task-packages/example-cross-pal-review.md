# Example Cross-Pal Review

Question: "这个团队设计是否合理？"

## Review Plan

| Reviewer | Context | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Product Pal | team goal and role list | role adequacy review |
| Quality Pal | team governance draft | risk review |
| Verifier Pal | all review outputs after independent phase | conflict check |

## Final Report

- Independent Review findings
- Owner Review summary
- Verifier Review
- Conflict Summary
- Final Recommendation

PalSmith coordinates the review boundary and quality gate. It does not replace the domain reviewers.
