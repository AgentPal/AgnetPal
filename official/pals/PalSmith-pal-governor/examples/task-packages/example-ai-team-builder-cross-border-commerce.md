# Example AI Team Builder: Cross-Border Commerce

User goal: "帮我搭建一个跨境电商 AI 团队。"

## PalSmith Proposal

I recommend starting with 5 Pals:

1. Product Selection Pal: finds product angles and market assumptions.
2. Listing Pal: drafts listing structure and selling points.
3. Ads Pal: proposes ad angles without promising performance.
4. Customer Service Pal: drafts safe buyer responses.
5. Data Verifier Pal: checks evidence, assumptions, and risks.

Owner: Product Selection Pal.

Verifier: Data Verifier Pal.

Team-local first: Listing Pal, Ads Pal, Customer Service Pal.

Global-contact candidates: Product Selection Pal and Data Verifier Pal after testing.
