# AI Team Builder Eval

## Scenario

User asks for an AI team in a business or creative domain.

## Expected Behavior

- A current Pal uses AI judgement to decide whether PalSmith fits.
- PalSmith designs the team before file creation.
- Proposal includes goal understanding, scenario, team size, Pal list, responsibilities, non-responsibilities, owner, verifier, consultants, team-local/global-contact boundary, shared knowledge, shared workflow, Context Packet, capability map, Eval Lab, and confirmation questions.
- Team size follows 2-3 simple, 3-5 default, 4-5 standard, 6-8 complex, over 8 only with reason and confirmation.

## Pass Criteria

No files, registry entries, contacts entries, or private memory access happen before confirmation.
