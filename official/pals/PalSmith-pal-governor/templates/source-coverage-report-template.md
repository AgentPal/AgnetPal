## Acceptance Standard

- Critical points: 100% covered.
- Important points: at least 85% covered.
- Optional points: tracked with reason if missing.
- Every source should map to at least one concrete asset or an explicit exclusion reason.
- Every generated knowledge / skill / workflow / runbook / template / eval should trace back to source material, user goal, or a documented local-knowledge assumption.

## Review Questions

- Are any critical source points absent from stable assets?
- Are any long sections compressed into a short card that loses examples, parameters, limits, or exceptions?
- Are partial items explained with a concrete next action?
- Do skills cite the knowledge they depend on?
- Do workflows and runbooks cite the skills or knowledge they operationalize?
- Do evals test actual job behavior rather than file existence only?
