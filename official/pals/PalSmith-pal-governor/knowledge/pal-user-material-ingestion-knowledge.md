# Pal User Material Ingestion Knowledge

## Concept

User material ingestion turns approved user documents, notes, examples, SOPs, and style samples into Pal assets without losing substantive content or privacy boundaries.

## Judgement Standards

- Inventory every source.
- Preserve source names and section anchors.
- Classify sections before writing.
- Keep examples, steps, exceptions, limits, and decision rules.
- Put uncertain material into review rather than dropping it.

## Examples

A user's sales playbook may become product knowledge, objection-handling runbook, email templates, qualification workflow, and eval cases.

## Counterexamples

- replacing a long source with a short overview
- omitting examples because they are inconvenient
- mixing private customer details into public knowledge
- writing unclassified material into the wrong asset

## Scope

Use whenever PalSmith asks the user to upload or approve materials.

## Convert To Assets

Material can become knowledge, skill, workflow, runbook, template, eval, example, reference, persona/voice, boundary, or decision.

## Common Errors

- no source inventory
- no classification table
- no preservation checklist
- no user confirmation for private material
