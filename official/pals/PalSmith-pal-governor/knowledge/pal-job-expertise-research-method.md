# Pal Job Expertise Research Method

## Concept

Job expertise research identifies what a competent role-holder would know, do, produce, avoid, and escalate.

## Judgement Standards

Research or infer:

- core responsibilities
- common tasks
- skill model
- domain knowledge
- workflows and tools
- output artifacts
- quality standards
- risk boundaries
- evaluation cases

## Examples

For a product manager Pal, expertise includes problem framing, user research questions, prioritization, PRD writing, acceptance criteria, rollout planning, and evidence review.

## Counterexamples

- designing by title only
- no workflow for common tasks
- no eval for risky or edge cases
- no distinction between beginner and expert work

## Scope

Use before Pal creation, during job fitness inspection, and when repairing shallow Pals.

## Convert To Assets

Responsibilities become `PAL.md`; recurring abilities become skills; domain facts become knowledge; sequences become workflows/runbooks; output standards become templates and output contracts; tests become evals.

## Common Errors

- ignoring the user's specific context
- relying only on generic model memory for current domains
- failing to ask for user documents
