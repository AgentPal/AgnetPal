# Pal Quality Criteria Knowledge

## Concept

Pal quality means the Pal can perform its intended job safely and coherently. File completeness is only the first layer.

## Judgement Standards

Evaluate:

- Role Fit
- Job Expertise Depth
- Skill Coverage
- Knowledge Coverage
- Workflow Coverage
- Output Contracts
- Eval Coverage
- Research Gap
- User Material Gap
- Collaboration Fit

## Examples

A strong customer support Pal has product policy knowledge, response templates, escalation workflow, risky-request refusals, tone rules, and evals for missing data and angry customers.

## Counterexamples

- root files exist but skills and knowledge are empty
- evals only check file presence
- no user material request for a personalized Pal
- no research gap despite a fast-changing domain

## Scope

Use for Pal Job Fitness Report, readiness review, Eval Lab, and publish quality gate.

## Convert To Assets

Missing dimensions become skill files, knowledge files, workflows, runbooks, templates, evals, or research/user-material task packages.

## Common Errors

- giving one total score without dimension detail
- calling a Pal publish-ready with incomplete evals
- treating inferred ability as proven ability
