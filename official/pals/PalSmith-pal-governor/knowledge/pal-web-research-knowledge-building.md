# Pal Web Research Knowledge Building

## Concept

Web research is a Runtime-executed evidence-gathering step that can improve Pal job expertise. PalSmith turns the evidence into candidate knowledge, skills, workflows, templates, and evals.

## Judgement Standards

- Define role-specific research questions.
- Record source URL, title, date accessed, publisher, and applicability.
- Mark uncertainty and recency limits.
- Keep candidate knowledge separate from stable knowledge until reviewed.
- Never claim live research when it did not run.

## Examples

For a website operator Pal, research may gather current content quality guidance, accessibility basics, search intent practices, analytics interpretation references, and publishing risks.

## Counterexamples

- one-line "add SEO knowledge" recommendation
- no sources
- outdated or irrelevant sources treated as authoritative
- live research claims without Runtime evidence

## Scope

Use for fast-changing domains, professional role design, publish readiness, and job expertise gap repair.

## Convert To Assets

Research becomes source inventory, candidate knowledge, skill requirements, workflow/runbook suggestions, output templates, evals, and risk boundaries.

## Common Errors

- promoting candidate knowledge without user review
- skipping source credibility
- mixing inference with sourced facts
