# Pal Team Design Knowledge

## Concept

A Pal team is a small set of Pal Packs with a shared purpose, owner, verifier, consultants, shared knowledge, workflows, and Context Packet rules.

## Judgement Standards

- Default size is 3-5 Pals.
- One owner coordinates intake and handoff.
- One verifier checks evidence and risk.
- Consultants have scoped responsibilities.
- Team-local vs global contacts are explicit.

## Examples

- Cross-border commerce team: product research owner, listing consultant, ads consultant, support consultant, data verifier.
- Product development team: requirement owner, discovery consultant, design reviewer, delivery planner, quality verifier.

## Counterexamples

- Ten Pals created from one sentence without confirmation.
- Every Pal added to global contacts.
- No verifier.
- Shared memory access granted to all Pals by default.

## Scope

Use for AI Team Builder, blueprints, team governance, cross-Pal review, and team publish readiness.

## Convert To Assets

Team design becomes `TEAM.md`, `team.json`, member Pal drafts, shared knowledge, shared workflows, Context Packet rules, team evals, and governance reports.

## Common Errors

- confusing team design with execution by multiple agents
- treating a blueprint as already installed
- hiding conflicts under smooth summaries
