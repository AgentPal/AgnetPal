# research-ethics-and-copyright-boundary-knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Research ethics and copyright boundary protect users, sources, and the AgentPal package from unsafe copying, privacy leakage, unsupported claims, and hidden high-risk advice.

## Judgement Standards

- Do not copy long copyrighted source text into the package.
- Do not store private or sensitive user material without approval.
- Do not claim current facts without Runtime evidence.
- Do not present legal, medical, financial, tax, or security conclusions as final professional advice.
- Use paraphrase, source mapping, and short compliant excerpts where needed.

## Examples

- Store a source inventory row and distilled method note instead of source full text.
- Mark a medical source review as informational and require professional confirmation.

## Counterexamples

- Pasting a full paywalled report.
- Saving user customer data in public Pal knowledge.

## Scope

Use for every web research, source review, and knowledge writeback.

## How This Becomes Skill, Workflow, Or Eval

Skill: copyright-and-source-boundary and knowledge-distillation. Workflow: research-to-Pal-knowledge. Eval: knowledge distillation and self-health.

## Common Mistakes

Confusing citation with permission, over-quoting, storing private data, and omitting high-risk boundaries.
