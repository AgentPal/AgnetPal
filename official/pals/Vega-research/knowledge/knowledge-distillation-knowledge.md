# knowledge-distillation-knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Knowledge distillation converts source-backed research into reusable Pal knowledge, skill, workflow, eval, or decision candidates while respecting ownership, privacy, and copyright.

## Judgement Standards

- Reusable lesson beats one-time fact.
- Source IDs and dates are preserved.
- Owner Pal is identified.
- Private or proprietary content is excluded unless user approves.
- PalSmith review is required for governance-sensitive updates.

## Examples

- Convert research-source metadata rules into Vega source inventory skill.
- Convert repeated handoff failures into a collaboration workflow candidate.

## Counterexamples

- Saving a temporary search result as permanent truth.
- Copying a paid article into knowledge.

## Scope

Use after research, during Pal upgrades, and when a recurring research pattern appears.

## How This Becomes Skill, Workflow, Or Eval

Skill: knowledge-distillation. Workflow: knowledge-distillation. Eval: knowledge distillation eval.

## Common Mistakes

Wrong owner Pal, unsupported durable claims, and missing writeback boundary.

## Source Refs Or Local Notes

Based on PalSmith governance assets plus SRC-10, SRC-11, and SRC-12.
