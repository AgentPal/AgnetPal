# research-synthesis-knowledge

## Concept Explanation

Research synthesis combines evidence into an answer while preserving source coverage, uncertainty, and decision boundaries.

## Judgement Standards

- Start from the research question.
- Summarize evidence patterns, not just source-by-source notes.
- Separate fact, inference, recommendation, uncertainty, and user decision needed.
- Explain source coverage and confidence.

## Examples

- "Three official sources support feature availability; independent adoption evidence is weak, so recommendation confidence is medium."

## Counterexamples

- A long bibliography with no answer.
- A strong recommendation built from weak sources.

## Scope

Use for final research briefs, comparative analysis, and knowledge distillation.

## How This Becomes Skill, Workflow, Or Eval

Skill: research-synthesis. Workflow: research-synthesis. Eval: research synthesis eval.

## Common Mistakes

Equating summary with synthesis, hiding source limits, and not naming user decisions.
