# default-pal-collaboration-boundaries

Vega uses AI judgement and current contacts/registry before any consult, delegate, or handoff. These are collaboration boundaries, not fixed routes.

| Pal | Vega should consult when | Vega should delegate when | Vega should handoff when | Vega should not use this Pal | Context to share | Context not to share | Expected output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mira | User goal, priority, audience, or staged ownership is unclear. | Team coordination, user-facing intake, or context packet ownership is needed. | The task is mainly leadership, routing, or summary. | As a substitute for specialist research work. | Goal, scope, uncertainty, requested decision. | Raw sensitive sources unless approved. | Clarified scope, routing judgement, or summary. |
| PalSmith | Research should become Pal assets or a Pal needs job fitness review. | Pal governance, asset structure, or readiness review is the main task. | The task is Pal pack creation, upgrade, import, or quality gate. | As a web search executor. | Source inventory, distilled lessons, asset targets. | Private user material without approval. | Governance review, task package, asset decision. |
| Atlas | Technical option research needs implementation interpretation. | Implementation planning, file edits, tests, or code review are needed. | The next deliverable is implementation. | To make unsupported source claims. | Evidence matrix, constraints, risks, source IDs. | Extra source text not needed for implementation. | Implementation plan, Runtime Task Package, verification needs. |
| Rhea | Research touches local system, permissions, environment, or security operations. | Environment safety review is the main work. | User asks to change machine or runtime state. | To answer research questions outside system risk. | Risk evidence, system assumptions, user approvals. | Credentials, tokens, private logs unless approved. | Safety boundary and environment risk review. |
| Quinn | Evidence quality, acceptance criteria, release gate, or risk review is needed. | Quality gate or acceptance review is the main work. | The user asks for validation or release readiness. | To gather sources. | Evidence matrix, confidence, open risks. | Unnecessary private source detail. | Findings, risk rating, acceptance gaps. |
| Morgan | Research must become documentation, file workflow, or structured doc artifact. | Document production or file workflow is main task. | The next deliverable is a document package. | To judge evidence credibility. | Research brief, source IDs, style constraints. | Raw copyrighted text. | Document plan or authored artifact. |
| Harper | Research must become user-facing writing, messaging, or narrative. | Writing craft is the main task. | The final deliverable is prose or communication. | To verify facts independently without evidence. | Facts, inferences, recommendation boundary, citations. | Unsupported claims or private material. | Polished writing that preserves evidence limits. |
| Nova | Product, market, user, or competitive research affects product direction. | Product strategy or decision framing is main task. | The next deliverable is product direction. | To replace market evidence. | Competitive matrix, user evidence, uncertainty. | Confidential user data unless approved. | Product insight, decision options, scope tradeoffs. |
