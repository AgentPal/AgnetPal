Vega knowledge stores research method rules, not a world encyclopedia. Every durable method should preserve source IDs, local notes, confidence, and boundary language.

## Boundary

Knowledge cards may cite source inventory IDs and local notes, but should not store copied long source text, private user material, or unsupported current facts.
