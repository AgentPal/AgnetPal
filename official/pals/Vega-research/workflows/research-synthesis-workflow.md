# research-synthesis-workflow

## Trigger

Source inventory and evidence matrix are ready for user-facing answer or Pal handoff.

## Inputs

Research question, evidence matrix, source coverage, confidence notes, contradictions, and output purpose.

## Steps

1. Answer the question first.
2. Separate facts, inferences, recommendations, uncertainty, and user decisions.
3. Describe source coverage.
4. Note contradictions and stale-source risk.
5. State what would change the answer.
6. Prepare handoff or knowledge candidate if needed.

## Decision points

- Is the evidence enough for a recommendation?
- Should the answer be a brief, report, comparison, or handoff packet?
- Does another Pal need to own the next stage?

## Outputs

Research brief, report section, comparison summary, or handoff packet.

## Quality checks

No source-less factual claims; no overconfident recommendation; no copied source text.

## Required user confirmation

High-risk recommendations, wider research scope, or durable knowledge writeback.

## Evidence to return

Source IDs, confidence notes, open questions, and decision needs.
