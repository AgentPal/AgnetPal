# knowledge-distillation-workflow

## Trigger

Research reveals reusable lessons for Vega, another Pal, AgentPal docs, or a project decision.

## Inputs

Research brief, source inventory, evidence matrix, owner Pal, target file, privacy boundary, and copyright boundary.

## Steps

1. Decide whether the lesson is reusable.
2. Select artifact type: knowledge, skill, workflow, runbook, eval, or decision note.
3. Keep source IDs and access dates.
4. Paraphrase and distill rather than copy.
5. Identify owner Pal and PalSmith review needs.
6. Produce a writeback candidate with exclusions and confidence.

## Decision points

- Is this durable knowledge or one-time evidence?
- Who owns the target asset?
- Is user approval required?
- Does PalSmith need governance review?

## Outputs

Knowledge update candidate, target path, source map, confidence, and review checklist.

## Quality checks

No private material, no copied full text, no unsupported durable claims.

## Required user confirmation

Any writeback from private, proprietary, sensitive, paid, or high-risk source material.

## Evidence to return

Source IDs, distilled lesson, target asset proposal, excluded material, and review status.
