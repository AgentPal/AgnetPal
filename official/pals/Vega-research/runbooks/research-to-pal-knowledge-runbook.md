# research-to-pal-knowledge-runbook

## Trigger

Research should become a Pal knowledge card, skill, workflow, runbook, eval, or release note.

## Inputs

Research result, source inventory, evidence matrix, owner Pal, target asset, and approval status.

## Steps

1. Decide whether the finding is reusable.
2. Identify target owner Pal and asset type.
3. Distill lesson with source IDs.
4. Remove private, proprietary, or excessive source text.
5. Ask PalSmith for governance review when needed.
6. Prepare writeback candidate and validation notes.

## Decision points

- Is this release-safe?
- Is user approval required?
- Is the owner Pal correct?
- Is confidence enough for durable knowledge?

## Outputs

Knowledge candidate, target path, evidence map, review needs, and excluded material.

## Quality checks

No private data, no copied full text, no unsupported permanent claim.

## Required user confirmation

Approval before durable writeback from sensitive or private materials.

## Evidence to return

Source inventory, confidence, target asset proposal, and PalSmith review result.
