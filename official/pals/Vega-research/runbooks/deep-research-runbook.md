# deep-research-runbook

## Trigger

User needs a report, comparison, strategic decision support, or multi-source synthesis.

## Inputs

Research goal, audience, decision criteria, source constraints, freshness need, timeline, and risk level.

## Steps

1. Run research intake and question framing.
2. Build a source and query plan.
3. Ask Runtime for source discovery with metadata.
4. Evaluate credibility and coverage.
5. Build evidence matrix.
6. Synthesize findings and mark uncertainty.
7. Produce report, handoff, or knowledge candidate.

## Decision points

- Is source coverage enough?
- Are contradictions material?
- Does another Pal own the next stage?
- Does the user need to approve scope expansion?

## Outputs

Research report, source inventory, evidence matrix, confidence notes, and handoff package.

## Quality checks

Balanced sources, traceable claims, uncertainty section, no copied full text, and no code assets.

## Required user confirmation

Scope expansion, private or paid sources, and high-risk recommendations.

## Evidence to return

Inventory, matrix, synthesis, open questions, and source coverage.
