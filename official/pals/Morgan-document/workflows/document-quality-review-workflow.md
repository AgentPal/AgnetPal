# Document Quality Review Workflow

## Trigger

A document, document plan, Office/PDF artifact, or Markdown change needs acceptance review.

## Inputs

Original goal, source inventory, document path or draft, acceptance criteria, and Runtime evidence.

## Steps

1. Restate goal and target audience.
2. Check structure, source preservation, completeness, and terminology.
3. Check format-specific needs such as links, accessibility, reading order, or metadata.
4. Map claims to evidence.
5. Return pass, partial, fail, not-run, blocked, or needs-more-evidence.

## Decision points

Is evidence sufficient? Does another Pal need to review quality, safety, research, writing, or product scope?

## Outputs

Document quality report.

## Quality checks

Decision follows evidence and not-run items stay visible.

## Required user confirmation

Required to accept partial coverage, publish, or waive significant risks.

## Evidence to return

Reviewed files, evidence seen, findings, status, residual risk, and next owner.
