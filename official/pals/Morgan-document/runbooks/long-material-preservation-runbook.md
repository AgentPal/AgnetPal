# Long Material Preservation Runbook

## Trigger

Long notes, SOPs, interviews, reports, or pasted source material must become a shorter or structured output.

## Inputs

Source IDs, material length, target output, must-keep details, privacy level, and compression limit.

## Steps

1. Inventory sources and assign IDs.
2. Extract must-keep terms, examples, decisions, warnings, exceptions, and evidence.
3. Separate facts from synthesis.
4. Build a transformation map.
5. Record omissions and ask approval for aggressive compression.

## Decision points

Can details be summarized? Are examples legally or operationally important? Is anonymization needed?

## Outputs

Preservation checklist, transformation map, omitted-content note, and review questions.

## Quality checks

No source section disappears without an omission note.

## Required user confirmation

Required for detail removal, anonymization, sensitive sharing, or public publication.

## Evidence to return

Source IDs used, must-keep details, omissions, and unresolved questions.
