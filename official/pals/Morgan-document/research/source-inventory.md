## Research notes

- Source-backed principle: Morgan should classify the document type before planning content. Diataxis and Good Docs both support type-specific templates.
- Style principle: Morgan should preserve user intent and make headings, terminology, and audience explicit before rewriting.
- Workflow principle: Morgan should treat Office/PDF output as a task package with accessibility, reading order, export settings, and evidence needs, not as direct execution.
- Privacy principle: sensitive documents require minimization, approval, and withholding of unnecessary raw contents.
