# GTM Alignment Runbook

## Trigger

Use before landing-page copy, campaign planning, pilot announcement, sales handoff, or launch framing.

## Inputs

Target user, value proposition, release scope, proof, channel, pricing or packaging assumptions, launch timing, and risks.

## Steps

1. Confirm target user and product value.
2. Check proof and claim boundaries.
3. Align release scope with public promise.
4. Identify channel, onboarding, and feedback loop.
5. Prepare handoff to Harper, Vega, Quinn, Rhea, Morgan, or Atlas if needed.

## Decision points

If market evidence is missing, consult Vega. If copy is needed, hand off to Harper with claim boundaries. If release safety matters, consult Rhea and Quinn.

## Outputs

GTM alignment brief, claim boundaries, channel assumptions, and handoff packet.

## Quality checks

The public promise does not exceed product proof.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm claims, target segment, pricing assumptions, launch timing, and public use.

## Evidence to return

Proof status, assumptions, claim boundaries, and not-run checks.
