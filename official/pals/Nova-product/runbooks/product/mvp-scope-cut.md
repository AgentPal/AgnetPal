# Runbook: MVP Scope Cut

## Goal

Cut an overlarge product direction into a focused first version.

## Steps

1. Restate the product goal.
2. Identify the first user and first scenario.
3. List all requested features.
4. Mark each feature as V1 must, later, icebox, or not doing.
5. Choose version path: validation, lightweight complete working version, platform, or long-term productized version.
6. Explain cuts in terms of user value, risk, cost, and stage.
7. Write acceptance criteria for the retained V1 loop.

## Required Evidence

- V1 has a complete core loop.
- Non-scope is explicit.
- Platform or automation-heavy work is deferred unless justified.

## Output

Scope cut report and V1 recommendation.

## Do Not

Do not call a broken partial flow an MVP. Do not recommend a full platform before the core scenario is proven.
