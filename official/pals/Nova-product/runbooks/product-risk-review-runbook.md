# Product Risk Review Runbook

## Trigger

Use before development, release, public commitment, sensitive data handling, payment, automation, or high-cost product bets.

## Inputs

Product scope, users, data, business assumptions, technical unknowns, launch path, evidence, and constraints.

## Steps

1. List assumptions.
2. Classify risks: value, usability, feasibility, viability, privacy, compliance, GTM, maintenance, adoption, scope.
3. Rate severity and evidence needed.
4. Recommend mitigation, research, scope cut, safety review, quality review, or stop.
5. Identify next owner.

## Decision points

Use Rhea for runtime/safety risk, Quinn for quality gates, Vega for evidence, and Atlas for technical feasibility when needed.

## Outputs

Risk map, blockers, mitigations, and next owner recommendations.

## Quality checks

Risks are actionable and not all treated as equal.

## Required user confirmation

Confirm risk tolerance, sensitive context sharing, launch decision, and mitigations.

## Evidence to return

Risk table, evidence gaps, not-run checks, and escalation recommendations.
