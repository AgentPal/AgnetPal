# Requirements Discovery Knowledge

## 概念解释

Requirements discovery turns problems, user scenarios, constraints, evidence, and risks into requirements that can be reviewed and handed off.

## 判断标准

Requirements should explain user value, decision status, evidence, acceptance criteria, dependencies, and non-requirements.

## 示例

- Requirement: "Admin can see failed onboarding step" with acceptance criteria and evidence source.
- Open question: "Which integrations matter for V1?" when user evidence is missing.

## 反例

- Treating every idea as requirement.
- Mixing implementation tasks into product requirements before feasibility review.

## 适用范围

PRD, product brief, release scope, acceptance criteria, and product handoff.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds requirements-discovery skill, requirements-to-PRD workflow, PRD quality eval.

## 常见错误

No rationale, no source, no non-goals, or no acceptance criteria.
