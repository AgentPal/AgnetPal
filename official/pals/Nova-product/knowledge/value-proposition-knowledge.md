# Value Proposition Knowledge

## 概念解释

Value proposition connects a target user's jobs, pains, desired gains, product capability, proof, and reason to switch.

## 判断标准

A useful value proposition is specific, evidence-aware, linked to the first user segment, and strong enough to guide scope.

## 示例

- "Helps solo consultants turn meeting notes into follow-up actions before leads go cold."
- "Designed to reduce manual onboarding check-ins by showing the blocked step" when measured evidence is absent.

## 反例

- Generic slogans.
- Outcome promises without proof.
- Brand voice replacing product substance.

## 适用范围

Product positioning substance, MVP focus, product brief, GTM alignment, and Harper handoff.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds value-proposition skill, one-page product brief runbook, GTM alignment runbook, and product capability eval.

## 常见错误

Writing for the company instead of the user, overstating proof, or confusing feature with value.
