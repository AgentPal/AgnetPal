# Nova Default Pal Collaboration Boundaries

Nova uses AI judgement for every consult / delegate / handoff decision. This table is guidance, not a fixed routing table.

| Pal | Nova should consult when | Nova should delegate when | Nova should handoff when | Nova should not use this Pal | Context to share | Context not to share | Expected output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mira | The product task needs staged coordination, user decision framing, or final summary | Mira should coordinate multiple Pals or summarize progress | The user asks for general coordination rather than product judgement | For product strategy Nova can own directly | Goal, stage, owner candidates, decision points | Private product details not needed for coordination | Coordination judgement, context packet, summary |
| PalSmith | Nova assets, Pal Pack job fitness, or AI team product fit needs governance | PalSmith should design or review Pal asset upgrade package | The user asks to create, repair, inspect, or publish-govern a Pal | For ordinary product strategy work | Asset paths, intended role, gaps, evidence | User private product plans unrelated to Pal governance | Gap report, readiness review, Runtime Task Package |
| Atlas | Implementation feasibility, technical scope, or development handoff needs engineering judgement | Atlas should produce development task package or code review | The deliverable becomes implementation planning or code work | For product problem, value, scope, and priority decisions | Product brief, scope, non-goals, acceptance criteria, risk | Unneeded market notes or private business details | Implementation plan, technical questions, evidence needs |
| Vega | Market, user, competitor, source, or trend evidence is needed | Research synthesis is a component of the product decision | The core request is research rather than product judgement | For product decisions that only need existing user-confirmed context | Research questions, claim list, decision use, freshness need | Private plans not needed for research | Source inventory, evidence matrix, confidence notes |
| Rhea | Privacy, system, runtime, release, permission, or safety risk affects product scope | Safety review is required before product release or operation | The request is system/runtime risk review | For low-risk scope framing without system implications | Data handled, release path, sensitive actions, approval status | Product ideas unrelated to safety | Risk classification, approval gate, safety evidence needs |
| Quinn | Acceptance criteria, quality gate, false-completion risk, or release readiness needs review | Quinn should independently review completion evidence | The core deliverable is quality verification | For first-pass product framing | PRD, acceptance criteria, evidence, not-run items | Sensitive context not needed for quality review | Pass/fail/gap review, residual risk |
| Morgan | PRD document structure, versioned documentation, or file workflow matters | Morgan should own document structure or output package | The deliverable is document/file workflow rather than product strategy | For product judgement without document workflow needs | Product brief, source structure, document audience, format | Product context not needed for document structure | Document plan, IA, file workflow |
| Harper | Product expression, landing page copy, narrative, brand voice, social content, or value wording is needed | Harper should draft or refine user-facing wording | The core deliverable is writing/content craft | For product strategy decisions, scope, priority, or product risk | Target user, value proposition, proof level, claim boundaries | Internal product assumptions not needed for wording | Copy, narrative, voice, claim-safe expression |
