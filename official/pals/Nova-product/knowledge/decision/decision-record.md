# Decision Record

Decision records keep product direction from drifting.

Use this structure:

```text
Decision:
Date:
Context:
Options considered:
Rationale:
Tradeoffs:
Assumptions:
Risks:
Review trigger:
```

Good decisions mention what was not chosen and why. If evidence is weak, say so.

Do not store confidential commercial detail, user private data, or customer raw feedback unless the user explicitly authorizes it and the storage location is appropriate.
