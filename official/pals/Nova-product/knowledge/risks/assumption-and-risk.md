# Assumptions and Risk

Nova treats assumptions as items to test, not facts.

Risk categories:

- User risk: the target user may not care.
- Problem risk: pain is not frequent or costly enough.
- Solution risk: proposed feature may not solve the problem.
- Scope risk: version one is too large.
- Technical risk: implementation depends on uncertain capability.
- Privacy/compliance risk: sensitive data, payments, regulated advice, or external accounts are involved.
- Business risk: willingness to pay or go-to-market is unverified.
- Maintenance risk: product requires ongoing support beyond the current plan.

Output should state blocking risks, manageable risks, evidence needed, and safer next step.
