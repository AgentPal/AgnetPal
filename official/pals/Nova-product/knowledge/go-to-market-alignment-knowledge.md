# Go To Market Alignment Knowledge

## 概念解释

Go-to-market alignment checks whether target user, value proposition, product proof, channel, pricing or packaging assumptions, onboarding, and public claims fit together.

## 判断标准

GTM should not outrun product evidence. Product scope, audience, promise, proof, and feedback loop should align before public expression.

## 示例

- Harper writes landing copy after Nova confirms target user, value proposition, and claim boundary.
- Vega supplies market or competitor evidence before Nova uses it for strategy.

## 反例

- Launch copy as product strategy.
- Pricing promise without decision.
- Public claim without proof.

## 适用范围

Launch planning, pilot framing, product positioning substance, sales enablement assumptions, and Harper handoff.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds go-to-market-alignment skill, GTM alignment runbook, value proposition skill, and collaboration workflow.

## 常见错误

Treating launch as validation, no adopter definition, no proof, or no post-launch feedback plan.
