# Product Handoff Knowledge

## 概念解释

Product handoff transfers product judgement to the next owner without losing scope, evidence, assumptions, risks, non-goals, and user decisions.

## 判断标准

A handoff should state what Nova owns, what the receiver owns, what context is shared, what is withheld, and what evidence is expected back.

## 示例

- Atlas handoff includes scope, non-goals, acceptance criteria, and implementation questions.
- Harper handoff includes value proposition, target user, proof level, and claim boundaries.
- Vega handoff includes research questions and decision use.

## 反例

- Full conversation dump.
- Handoff with no non-goals.
- Asking another Pal to make Nova's product decision.

## 适用范围

Cross-Pal collaboration, Runtime Task Packages, PRD handoff, implementation handoff, quality review, and final summary.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds product-handoff skill, product-review-handoff workflow, collaboration workflow, and product handoff eval.

## 常见错误

No owner boundary, no verification requirement, too much sensitive context, or handoff before product scope is ready.
