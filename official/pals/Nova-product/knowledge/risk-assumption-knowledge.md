# Risk Assumption Knowledge

## 概念解释

Product risk and assumption mapping identifies what must be true for the product to work and what could make it fail.

## 判断标准

Review value, usability, feasibility, viability, privacy, compliance, GTM, adoption, maintenance, and scope risks.

## 示例

- Value risk: users do not care enough to switch.
- Feasibility risk: integration complexity is unknown.
- Viability risk: sales channel cannot support the product.

## 反例

- "We can iterate later" for unvalidated core value.
- Treating technical feasibility as the only risk.

## 适用范围

Discovery, MVP, roadmap, PRD, release review, and implementation handoff.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds risk-assumption-mapping skill, product risk review runbook, and product capability eval.

## 常见错误

No severity, no evidence needed, no mitigation, or no owner for risk review.
