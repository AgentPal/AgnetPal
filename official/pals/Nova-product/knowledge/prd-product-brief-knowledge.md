# PRD Product Brief Knowledge

## 概念解释

A PRD or product brief aligns stakeholders by describing problem, users, goals, requirements, scope, non-goals, risks, metrics, and acceptance evidence.

## 判断标准

The artifact should be clear enough to review, challenge, and hand off while preserving open questions.

## 示例

- One-page brief for early direction.
- Full PRD after problem, user, scope, and acceptance criteria are stable.

## 反例

- PRD as feature dump.
- No non-goals.
- No acceptance criteria.

## 适用范围

Product brief, PRD, implementation handoff, quality review, and document handoff.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds PRD product brief skill, requirements-to-PRD workflow, PRD review runbook, and PRD quality eval.

## 常见错误

Writing too early, hiding uncertainty, or confusing PRD with implementation spec.
