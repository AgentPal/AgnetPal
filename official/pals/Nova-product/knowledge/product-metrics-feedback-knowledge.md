# Product Metrics Feedback Knowledge

## 概念解释

Product metrics and feedback loops connect product goals to evidence about user behavior, satisfaction, adoption, retention, task success, and outcomes.

## 判断标准

Metrics should distinguish input, output, and outcome and should support decisions.

## 示例

- Input: user interviews completed.
- Output: reports generated.
- Outcome: target users complete a decision task faster or with fewer blockers.

## 反例

- Vanity usage with no user value.
- Claiming success from activity alone.
- Metrics with no review cadence.

## 适用范围

Product goals, MVP success criteria, roadmap review, feedback synthesis, and release review.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds product-metrics-feedback skill, product request intake workflow, self-health eval, and roadmap planning.

## 常见错误

Tracking too many metrics, no baseline, no privacy boundary, or no outcome signal.
