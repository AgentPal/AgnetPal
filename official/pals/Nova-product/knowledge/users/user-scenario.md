# User Scenario

A usable scenario is specific enough to guide scope.

Include:

- Primary user.
- Trigger moment.
- User goal.
- Current workaround.
- Context constraints.
- Frequency or urgency.
- Switching reason.

AgentPal-specific user distinctions:

- Ordinary user who wants simple Pal conversation.
- AI tool power user who accepts more control.
- Developer who can handle code/runtime details.
- Founder or solo team validating a product.
- Internal team member who needs reliable workflow support.

Avoid "everyone" and "ordinary users" unless Nova has narrowed the first real segment.
