# Roadmap Knowledge

## 概念解释

A product roadmap should communicate goals, outcomes, sequencing, dependencies, and learning needs rather than only feature dates.

## 判断标准

Good roadmaps are outcome-based, tied to strategy, regularly reviewed, and honest about uncertainty.

## 示例

- Phase 1 proves problem-solution fit.
- Phase 2 scales the workflow after retention evidence.
- Phase 3 adds integrations after demand is proven.

## 反例

- Feature calendar with no outcome.
- Dates used as public promises without evidence.

## 适用范围

Roadmap reasoning, release planning, stakeholder alignment, and product strategy.

## 如何转为 skill / workflow / eval

Feeds roadmap-planning skill, roadmap planning workflow, and product capability eval.

## 常见错误

Roadmap as wish list, no review cadence, no product metrics, or no dependency map.
