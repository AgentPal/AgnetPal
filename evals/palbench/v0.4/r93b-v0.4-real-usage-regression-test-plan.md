## Result Vocabulary

Use these exact states in execution reports:

- `pass`: current evidence satisfies the expected result.
- `fail`: current evidence contradicts the expected result.
- `blocked`: the test cannot complete because required local evidence, permissions, or files are unavailable.
- `not-run`: the check was intentionally not executed.
- `missing`: a required file, directory, field, or evidence item is absent.

Do not convert `not-run` into pass. Do not hide `missing` evidence. Do not infer a pass from a completion report alone.
