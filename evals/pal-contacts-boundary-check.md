# Pal Contacts Boundary Check

Manual checklist for contacts and registry boundaries.

- [ ] `workspace/organization/contacts/pals.json` includes Mira plus Atlas, Vega, Rhea, Quinn, Morgan, Harper, and Nova.
- [ ] `workspace/organization/contacts/PAL_CONTACTS.md` includes the same Pal set.
- [ ] Ordinary messages go to Mira.
- [ ] Specialist Pals do not listen by default.
- [ ] `/pal Atlas`, `/pal Vega`, `/pal Rhea`, `/pal Quinn`, `/pal Morgan`, `/pal Harper`, `/pal Nova`, and `/pal PalSmith` resolve by display name.
- [ ] Tools, models, MCP servers, plugins, non-Pal runtimes, and raw repositories do not appear as Pal contacts.
