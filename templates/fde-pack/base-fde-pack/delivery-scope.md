# Delivery Scope

## In scope

- `[Reusable discovery step]`
- `[Reusable review step]`
- `[Reusable checklist or template]`
- `[Reusable verification activity]`

## Out of scope

- customer-specific final decisions without human review;
- credential handling;
- external API calls;
- connector setup;
- automated execution;
- legal, medical, tax, financial, or compliance advice without qualified review.

## Inputs

Use only public-safe or explicitly approved private inputs. Customer-private inputs must remain outside the reusable pack.

## Outputs

Outputs may include checklists, review notes, delivery reports, task packages, or verification records. These are no-code governance artifacts.
