# Customer-Private Boundary

## Rule

Reusable FDE Packs must not contain customer-private assets.

## Customer-private examples

- credentials, tokens, passwords, API keys, cookies, or secrets;
- real contracts;
- real invoices;
- real financial statements;
- customer ledgers;
- customer databases;
- sensitive personal information;
- medical, legal, tax, financial, employment, or compliance evidence;
- private project memory;
- private reports;
- customer-specific business rules.

## Storage

Customer-private assets belong in approved private records, central project records, customer-private delivery folders, or runtime-private evidence locations.

They do not belong in public templates or examples.

## Review

If sensitivity is unclear, mark the material `requires-review` and keep it out of the reusable pack.
