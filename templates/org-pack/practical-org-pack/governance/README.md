# Governance

## Purpose

Use this directory for reusable governance rules, decision records, review
checkpoints, and change-control guidance.

## What Belongs Here

- Approval boundaries.
- Review checklists.
- Decision-record templates.
- Escalation checkpoints.
- Ownership and evidence rules.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Automatic central roster edits.
- Private decision records.
- Credentials or private evidence.
- Claims that legal, compliance, or release approval has already happened.

## Relationship To Pal Assets

Governance may recommend PalSmith, Quinn, Mira, or other Pals for review as AI
judgement inputs only.

## Relationship To Project Records

Real project governance outcomes belong in private project records or approved
reports.

## Public Safety Boundary

Publish reusable policy, not private outcomes.
