# Roles

## Purpose

Use this directory for reusable organization roles and collaboration notes.

## What Belongs Here

- Role descriptions.
- Responsibility boundaries.
- Collaboration patterns.
- Recommended Pal perspectives as AI judgement inputs.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Copied Pal Pack bodies.
- Copied central roster files.
- Fixed task-to-Pal assignments.
- Private staff names, customer names, or private org charts.

## Relationship To Pal Assets

Roles may suggest Pals or Pal Skills to consider, but actual Pal selection still
comes from AI judgement using the current central roster and task context.

## Relationship To Project Records

Project-specific owners, staffing, and stakeholder facts belong in private
project records, not this reusable directory.

## Public Safety Boundary

Use generic role language and de-identified examples.
