# Runbooks

## Purpose

Use this directory for reusable exception-handling or concrete operation guides.

## What Belongs Here

- Symptoms and triggers.
- Checks and stop conditions.
- Escalation notes.
- Verification evidence requirements.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Broad strategy that belongs in workflows.
- CLI recipes presented as execution claims.
- Connector secrets or system credentials.
- Customer-specific incident evidence.

## Relationship To Pal Assets

Runbooks may point to Pal-owned runbooks as candidates, but they must not copy
Pal-owned bodies into this Org Pack.

## Relationship To Project Records

Customer incidents, project-specific failures, and private evidence belong in
private records.

## Public Safety Boundary

Use public-safe examples and avoid sensitive system names.
