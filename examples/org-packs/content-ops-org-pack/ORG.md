# Content Ops Org Pack

## Purpose

This Org Pack describes a reusable content operations AI organization pattern.

It is designed for teams that need recurring content planning, research
briefing, drafting, editing, publishing readiness review, and retrospective
learning while keeping customer-private materials out of the reusable package.

## Reusable Roles

- Intake and coordination lead.
- Research and source review partner.
- Document and asset workflow partner.
- Writing and editorial craft partner.
- Quality and release-readiness reviewer.

These roles can inform Pal selection, but they do not assign work
deterministically.

## Reusable Workflows

- Content request intake.
- Brief and source pack preparation.
- Draft and edit loop.
- Publishing readiness review.
- Post-publication learning candidate review.

## Governance

This Org Pack expects:

- source and citation uncertainty to stay visible;
- private customer materials to stay out of reusable examples;
- publishing decisions to use explicit verification;
- Pal recommendations to remain AI judgement inputs.

## Runtime Boundary

This Org Pack does not publish content, call APIs, operate social accounts,
install tools, scan repositories, or validate external systems by itself. A host
Runtime may execute approved work only through a separate task package with
current evidence.
