# Reusable Assets

## Advisory discovery checklist

- Confirm business model at a high level without recording private customer facts in this reusable pack.
- Identify accounting system category without storing system credentials or customer identifiers.
- Confirm reporting cadence and decision audience.
- Identify whether tax, payroll, audit, or regulated reporting is in scope.
- Mark missing evidence as `missing`.
- Mark unreviewed customer data as `requires-human-review`.

## Monthly close readiness checklist

- Bank and card reconciliation status reviewed by a qualified person.
- Accounts receivable ageing reviewed.
- Accounts payable ageing reviewed.
- Payroll posting status reviewed where applicable.
- Inventory or cost-of-goods process reviewed where applicable.
- Unusual transactions flagged for human review.
- Month-end adjustments reviewed before customer use.

## Bookkeeping hygiene review checklist

- Chart of accounts consistency reviewed.
- Uncategorized transactions reviewed.
- Duplicate vendor or customer records checked.
- Personal and business expense separation reviewed.
- Suspense or clearing accounts reviewed.
- Prior-period changes flagged for human review.

## Management report preparation outline

- Confirm reporting period.
- Confirm intended audience.
- Prepare high-level sections such as revenue, margin, operating expense, cash position, and open questions.
- Keep customer-private figures in the private engagement record, not in this reusable example.

## Update policy note

Accounting, tax, payroll, and reporting requirements can change. Review this pack before real customer use and whenever rules, jurisdictions, accounting standards, or engagement scope changes.
