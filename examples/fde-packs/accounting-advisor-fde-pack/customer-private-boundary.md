# Customer-Private Boundary

## Do not include

This public-safe example must not include:

- real customer names;
- company identifiers;
- real tax identification numbers;
- real invoices;
- real receipts;
- real bank statements;
- real payroll records;
- real ledgers;
- real financial statements;
- customer accounting system URLs or IDs;
- credentials, tokens, passwords, API keys, cookies, or secrets;
- private reports;
- customer-specific accounting judgements.

## Where customer-private material belongs

Customer-private accounting material belongs in an approved private engagement record, central project record, or customer-private evidence folder.

It must not be copied into this reusable FDE Pack.

## Human review

Customer-specific accounting, tax, payroll, audit, or financial reporting conclusions require qualified human review.
