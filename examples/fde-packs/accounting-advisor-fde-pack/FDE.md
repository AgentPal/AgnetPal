# Accounting Advisor FDE Pack

## Purpose

This pack captures a reusable accounting advisor delivery pattern for discovery, monthly close readiness review, bookkeeping hygiene review, and management-report preparation.

## Intended users

- accounting advisors;
- bookkeeping consultants;
- small-business finance operations teams;
- AgentPal maintainers designing public-safe expert delivery examples.

## Delivery promise

This pack provides reusable no-code advisory checklists, scope boundaries, and verification criteria.

It does not provide customer-specific tax, audit, payroll, or financial reporting conclusions without qualified human review.

## High-risk boundary

Accounting and tax work can affect legal, financial, and compliance outcomes. Use this pack only with human review, current jurisdictional context, and customer-private evidence kept outside the reusable pack.
