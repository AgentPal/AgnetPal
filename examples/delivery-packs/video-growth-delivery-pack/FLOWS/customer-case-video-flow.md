# Customer Case Video Flow

## Flow purpose

Create a short customer-case video structure from approved public customer material or clearly simulated example material.

## Inputs

- approved customer permission status;
- public customer quote or simulated quote;
- before/after context;
- measurable result boundary;
- brand and legal review notes.

## Steps

1. Material Manager Pal classifies every source as public-safe, private, permission-required, or simulated.
2. Research Pal identifies the audience lesson and uncertainty.
3. Scriptwriter Pal drafts a case arc: situation, challenge, change, outcome, lesson, CTA.
4. Brand Guardian Pal checks consent, privacy, and claim risk.
5. Video QA Pal checks captions, attribution notes, and missing proof.
6. Publishing Pal prepares manual handoff notes with approval blockers.
7. Growth Review Pal records learning candidates after approved evidence exists.

## Outputs

- customer case outline;
- permission and source classification table;
- script draft;
- claim and privacy review notes;
- publishing blocker list.

## Human review

Customer permission, legal or brand approval, and final publication approval are required.

## Boundary

Real customer-private material must not be placed in this reusable example. Unknown permission is treated as blocked.
