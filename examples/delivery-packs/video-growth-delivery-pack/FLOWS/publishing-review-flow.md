# Publishing Review Flow

## Flow purpose

Create a manual review gate before any drafted video asset is published.

## Inputs

- script or caption draft;
- source material list;
- target platform profile note;
- claim boundary;
- brand review notes;
- approval owner.

## Steps

1. Material Manager Pal confirms source classification and permission status.
2. Brand Guardian Pal reviews voice, claim safety, and restricted topics.
3. Video QA Pal checks readability, caption length, accessibility, and production notes.
4. Publishing Pal prepares platform-specific handoff notes without connecting to any account.
5. Growth Review Pal defines what evidence should be reviewed after manual publication.
6. Human reviewer approves, requests revision, or blocks publication.

## Outputs

- publish-ready review checklist;
- blocker list;
- handoff notes;
- post-publication evidence request.

## Human review

Publication cannot proceed without a human reviewer marking the draft approved.

## Boundary

This flow is not an approval automation, connector, platform client, or auto-publishing tool.
