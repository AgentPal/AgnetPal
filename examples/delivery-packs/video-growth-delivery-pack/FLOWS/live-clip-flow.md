# Live Clip Flow

## Flow purpose

Turn a manually selected live-stream segment into a short clip concept, caption draft, and publication review package.

## Inputs

- human-selected clip timestamp or simulated clip note;
- speaker permission status;
- topic summary;
- product or offer context;
- sensitive-topic notes.

## Steps

1. Material Manager Pal records clip source, permission status, and reusable/private boundary.
2. Research Pal identifies audience relevance and possible context loss.
3. Scriptwriter Pal drafts title, caption, opening text, and CTA options.
4. Brand Guardian Pal checks whether the clip preserves meaning and avoids unsafe claims.
5. Video QA Pal reviews caption clarity, accessibility, and missing context.
6. Publishing Pal prepares platform-profile handoff notes for manual posting.
7. Growth Review Pal records learning candidates after human-approved evidence exists.

## Outputs

- clip brief;
- title and caption options;
- context-risk note;
- review checklist;
- manual publishing handoff.

## Human review

Human review is required for speaker permission, edit context, final caption, and publishing decision.

## Boundary

This flow does not download streams, edit media, publish clips, or store private recordings.
