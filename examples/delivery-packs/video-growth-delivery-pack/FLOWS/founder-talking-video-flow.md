# Founder Talking Video Flow

## Flow purpose

Prepare founder-style talking-head content that explains a product belief, customer problem, or product decision without using private customer data.

## Inputs

- founder viewpoint;
- approved product context;
- audience concern;
- tone notes;
- restricted claims;
- optional public reference links.

## Steps

1. Research Pal identifies audience objections and public comparison points.
2. Scriptwriter Pal drafts a 45-90 second outline with hook, story, insight, proof boundary, and CTA.
3. Brand Guardian Pal checks voice authenticity and removes unsupported claims.
4. Material Manager Pal records which notes are public-safe and which require approval.
5. Video QA Pal checks pace, caption friendliness, and review issues.
6. Publishing Pal creates profile-specific handoff notes for manual publishing.
7. Growth Review Pal captures lessons only after approved performance evidence exists.

## Outputs

- founder talking outline;
- script draft;
- recording prompts;
- review issue list;
- publishing handoff notes.

## Human review

The founder or approved brand owner must review final wording before recording.

## Boundary

This flow does not synthesize a real founder voice, impersonate a person, call publishing APIs, or store private interview notes in this reusable pack.
