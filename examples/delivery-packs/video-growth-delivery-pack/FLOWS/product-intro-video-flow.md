# Product Intro Video Flow

## Flow purpose

Turn one public-safe product feature or offer into short-video scripts, shot notes, review checkpoints, and publishing handoff notes.

## Inputs

- product name or public offer name;
- audience segment;
- one approved value proposition;
- allowed proof points;
- forbidden claim notes;
- source material inventory.

## Steps

1. Research Pal summarizes the audience pain, competing hooks, and uncertainty.
2. Scriptwriter Pal drafts three script variants: problem-solution, demo-first, and objection-first.
3. Brand Guardian Pal checks tone, claim boundaries, and required disclaimers.
4. Material Manager Pal maps each script line to available footage or source material.
5. Video QA Pal checks clarity, captions, accessibility, and review readiness.
6. Publishing Pal prepares handoff notes for human publishing review.
7. Growth Review Pal records learning candidates after human-approved publication results exist.

## Outputs

- three script variants;
- shot and material notes;
- claim review notes;
- publishing handoff checklist;
- post-publication review placeholder.

## Human review

Human review is required before any script becomes a recording or publishing instruction.

## Boundary

This flow is a no-code Flow Pack. It does not call a platform, fetch analytics, publish media, or create deterministic Pal routes.
