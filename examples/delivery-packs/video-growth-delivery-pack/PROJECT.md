# Project

## Project name

Video Growth Delivery Pack example.

## Business context

A content team wants repeatable support for short-video growth workflows without connecting to publishing accounts or storing customer-private content in reusable assets.

## Audience

- content lead;
- founder or spokesperson;
- video editor;
- brand reviewer;
- publishing operator;
- growth reviewer.

## Simulated source areas

- `materials/product-intro/`: missing public product facts;
- `materials/founder-notes/`: simulated talking points;
- `materials/customer-cases/`: blocked until permission is confirmed;
- `materials/live-clips/`: simulated clip notes only;
- `materials/publishing-profiles/`: profile notes only.

These paths are directory notes, not files created by this example.

## Risks

- unsupported product claims;
- customer permission ambiguity;
- private footage leakage;
- platform account credential handling;
- route-map misuse of candidate Pals;
- publication without human review.

## Launch plan

1. Confirm missing business facts.
2. Classify source material as public-safe, private, permission-required, or simulated.
3. Run the product intro flow first.
4. Use the first task package to draft scripts.
5. Hold human review before recording or publishing.
6. Record post-publication evidence only after approved manual publication.

## Data boundary

Reusable Delivery Pack files must not contain real customer data, credentials, raw analytics, private meetings, private footage, or external project `.agentpal/` payloads.
