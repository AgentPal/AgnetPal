# Training

## Audience

This training note is for a content team learning how to operate the Video Growth Delivery Pack manually with human review.

## Operating rules

- Treat every candidate Pal as a judgement input, not an automatic assignment.
- Keep customer-private materials out of reusable pack files.
- Mark unknown permissions as blocked.
- Keep publishing platforms as handoff notes only.
- Do not store credentials, tokens, cookies, or private keys.
- Keep final content in `requires-human-review` until a human approves it.

## Suggested first training run

1. Read `DELIVERY.md`.
2. Review `PROJECT.md` for missing information.
3. Open `FLOWS/product-intro-video-flow.md`.
4. Run `TASKS/task-001-three-product-video-scripts.md` manually.
5. Use `ACCEPTANCE.md` before any recording or publishing.
6. Record only public-safe learning candidates in `REPORTS/`.

## Escalation

Escalate to a human owner when claims, permissions, private source materials, platform access, or customer references are involved.
