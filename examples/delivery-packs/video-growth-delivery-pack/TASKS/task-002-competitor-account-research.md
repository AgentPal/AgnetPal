# Task 002: Competitor Account Research

## Task type

Task Package example.

## Goal

Prepare a public-source competitor account research brief for short-video topic planning.

## Owner judgement input

Candidate owner: Research Pal, with Brand Guardian Pal and Growth Review Pal as review perspectives. This is not an automatic route.

## Inputs

- competitor account list: missing;
- target platforms: profile notes only;
- time window: last 30 days, if a human provides public links;
- private analytics: not available.

## Steps

1. Ask for or receive public competitor account links.
2. Record the evidence source for each observation.
3. Classify each observation as fact, inference, or hypothesis.
4. Summarize recurring hooks, formats, CTAs, and audience questions.
5. Note uncertainty and missing evidence.
6. Provide topic ideas for human review.

## Deliverables

- public-source evidence table;
- hook and format summary;
- uncertainty list;
- topic idea list;
- human review note.

## Acceptance

- all observations cite public sources or are marked missing;
- no credentials, scraping instructions, private analytics, or platform tokens are requested;
- recommendations are labeled as inference where appropriate;
- final state is `requires-human-review`.

## Boundary

This task package does not fetch accounts automatically, scrape platforms, call external APIs, or store private competitor intelligence.
