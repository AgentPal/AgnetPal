## Reusable Combined Assets

The combined pack adds:

- reference-level composition guidance;
- PalSmith audit notes;
- customer-private boundary notes for the combined scenario;
- thin binding relationship notes;
- verification checklist for combined Org/FDE use.

## Excluded Private Assets

Do not place these in the reusable combined pack:

- real customer books, invoices, receipts, contracts, ledgers, tax materials,
  payroll records, bank statements, or financial statements;
- customer names, account names, system IDs, internal URLs, screenshots, project
  facts, private reports, private meeting content, or private business rules;
- credentials, tokens, cookies, passwords, private keys, API keys, connection
  strings, or secret-like values.
